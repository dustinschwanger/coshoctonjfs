  • Child & Adult Protective Services

    Emergency: To report child abuse or neglect, call 740-622-1020 (Mon-Fri, 7am-4pm) or 740-622-2411 (after hours - Sheriff's Department). For adult abuse (age 60+), call 740-622-1020.

    Child Protective Services

    Mission: To bridge the gap between parents and their children while maintaining the safety and well-being of the children involved.

    How to Report: Call 740-622-1020 during business hours or 740-622-2411 after hours (Sheriff's Department will contact on-call worker).

    Types of Abuse: Physical abuse, sexual abuse, emotional abuse, neglect, and abandonment.

    Confidentiality: Reports can be made anonymously. Your information remains confidential.

    Adult Protective Services

    Assists vulnerable adults age 60+ who face danger, cannot protect themselves, and lack adequate support.

    To Report Elder Abuse: Call 740-622-1020

    Types of Elder Abuse: Physical, sexual, neglect, exploitation, emotional abuse, abandonment, and self-neglect.

    Foster Care & Adoption

    Become a Foster Parent: Age 21+, single or married, homeowners or renters welcome. Training, medical coverage, and payments provided.

    Adoption Services: Over 3,200 children in Ohio await adoptive families. Financial support programs available including adoption assistance and subsidies.

    Contact: 740-622-1020 for foster care and adoption information.

    Kinship Care

    Family members or close friends can provide temporary care when children cannot remain home. Kinship Permanency Incentive Program (KPIP) available for legal custody/guardianship.
